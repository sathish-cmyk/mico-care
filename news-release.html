News Release

Teachers Trained to Respond Effectively to Autium Spectrum Disorder in the Classroom
June 2, 2023-In response to the rapidly rising prevalence rate of autism presentation in children currently being assessed at the Mico CARE Centre, which currently stands at 3 in every 10 cases, the Centre recently collaborated with Caribbean Support for Education (CASE), to conduct two Autism Teacher Training Workshops. The workshops which were in high demand, saw over one hundred teachers being trained across the two sessions which were held at the Centre's Manhattan Road location in Kingston.
The workshops were facilitated by Mrs. Deborah Santiago, Autism Coach and Founder of CASE, based in Fort Lauderdale, USA. She is seen here actively demonstrating to a captive audience of educators, techniques and strategies used to effectively teach and communicate with children on the autism spectrum in the classroom.
