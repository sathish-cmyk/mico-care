Publications and Research
In addition to hosting various informational and training workshops and seminars for teachers and parents, the Centre also produces various publications. These include an annual publication, CARE for Special Children as well as booklets and brochures and research studies, covering relevant areas affecting child development or teaching and learning.
On average Mico CARE Centre provides diagnostic assessment to close to 2000 children each year and provides intervention services to about 380 of these students annually. Through a robust public education programme, the Centre also provides capacity building opportunities to teachers and parents in Jamaica as well as in other countries across the English-Speaking Caribbean.
